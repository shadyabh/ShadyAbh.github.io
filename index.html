About Me

Welcome to my personal website!

I am a Research Scientist at Samsung Research. I received my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Ben-Gurion University of the Negev in 2017, and my Ph.D. (direct track) from the Department of Electrical Engineering at Tel Aviv University in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Raja Giryes.
My primary research interests lie in deep learning, with a particular focus on inverse problems, generative modeling, and self-supervised learning. Recently, I have also developed a strong interest in natural language processing and state-space models.
