About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I'm a passionate Ph.D. student at Tel Aviv University, specializing in deep learning, with a focus on inverse problems, generative modeling, signal processing, and computer vision. Additionally, I am a research scientist at Intel Corporation.

Research Interests

My primary research interests revolve around improving deep learning methodologies through the integration of signal processing techniques, particularly in the areas of inverse problems and generative modeling. Additionally, I am actively engaged in the development of state-of-the-art real-time video super-resolution algorithms at Intel Corporation.
